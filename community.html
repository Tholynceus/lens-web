HOW IT WORKS

from one tag to everyone's screen

01

spot something

you find a dev who rugged, a clean builder worth trusting, or a wallet worth flagging, open their x profile with lens active

02

add a label

hit the + label button in the panel, pick a tag and add evidence, your submission joins the community pool for that account

03

everyone sees it

once a label passes the confirmation threshold, every lens user sees it the moment they open that profile

KEEPING IT HONEST

how we stop abuse

a reputation system only works if the reputation is real, these rules keep tags trustworthy

3+

confirmation threshold

a tag needs at least three independent confirmations before it shows publicly, one person can't brand someone alone

!

evidence encouraged

tags backed by a link or transaction carry more weight and surface higher than unsupported ones

x

abuse gets banned

false or malicious tagging removes your ability to tag, the system protects people, it isn't a weapon

~

disputes are reviewed

anyone can dispute a tag from the panel, disputed labels are reviewed and removed if they don't hold up
